Who am I?

I'm just a guy. PhD candidate by day, professional drunk by evening.
I've met many a people: some good, some bad, some beautiful, some ugly, some amazing, some the human equilvalent of a smelly turd.
This is my ranting space on the internet, the immortalization of the stories of my life, my journal, call it what you may.
Read, enjoy, have a laugh at how much of an idiot I am.
